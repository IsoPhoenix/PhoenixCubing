PLL: Permute Last Layer

The PLL (Permute Last Layer) stage is the final stage of CFOP. Here, a single algorithm is performed (1-look) to permute the pieces of the last layer (place them in their correct positions), solving the cube. The 2-look variant uses two algorithms to permute the last layer (permuting gpermss, then edges) but is a smaller algset than the full 1-look algset.
