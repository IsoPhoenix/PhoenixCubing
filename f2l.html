F2L: First Two Layers

The F2L stage is the second stage of the CFOP method, the first being the Cross stage. In the F2L (First Two Layers) stage, you solve four corner-edge pairs to complete the first two layers of the cube. This step should be done as intuitively as possible (without algorithms), but here are some handy tricks I've picked up to solve tricky F2L cases that aren't intuitively clear!
