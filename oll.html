OLL: Orient Last Layer

The OLL (Orient Last Layer) stage is the third stage of CFOP. Here, a single algorithm is performed (1-look) to orient the pieces of the last layer (most commonly, solving the yellow side if you started on white cross). The 2-look variant uses two algorithms to orient the last layer (orienting edges, then corners) but is a smaller algset than the full 1-look algset.
